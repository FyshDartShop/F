Rules

You must agree to pay for the stock blaster upfront
once the first upgrade component arrives.


Paint and color schemes are of no additional charge,
and do not count as blaster components or parts.


3D Printing attachments is sold upon request.


You must provide the blaster's shipping to me
if the blaster cannot be found on shelves.


Note that speeding up the process may take time,
as parts from Worker or Artifact will take a
long time to ship.
